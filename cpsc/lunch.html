To install, drag this link: EzTasteNutrition into your bookmarks bar.

Or create a new bookmark with the following "url":

javascript:(function() { var scr = document.createElement('script'); scr.setAttribute('type', 'text/javascript'); scr.setAttribute('src', 'https://breadfu.com/cpsc/lunch.js'); document.head.appendChild(scr);})();void(0);

Click on the bookmarklet once you've gone into the menu page of tastenutrition (sometimes you will have to open one day's lunch to get things all in place for it to work). It will ask you whether your child wants milk or not. When it's active the background goes from standard white to a very light yellow and there's a link you can click on for each day's menu options which avoids the need to pick the item, pick the right milk row, and click Submit.

Enjoy!
